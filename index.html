Number of Points:

Whenever you click the cookie you produce more points!

When you reach 50 point, you will get 3 points every time you click on the cookie.
When you reach 500 point, you will get 10 points every time you click on the cookie.
When you reach 1,000 point, you will get 25 points every time you click on the cookie.
When you reach 100,000 point, you will get 1000 points every time you click on the cookie.
